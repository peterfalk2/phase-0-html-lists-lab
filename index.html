Okay, so we've got our grilled cheese ingredients, but what about the steps required to make a grilled cheese? Steps to a recipe need to be in order, otherwise we may end up with burnt cheese covered in bread and topped with a square of butter. The steps to making a basic grilled cheese would be:
  1. Spread butter on bread and frying pan
  2. Place bread in frying pan and fry
  3. Add cheese on top of bread
  4. Cover with second slice of bread
  5. Turn over and fry for 2 minutes
